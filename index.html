Paper Crafts Projects

🌸 Paper Flower Bouquet

Easy

📋 Materials Needed:

• Colored paper • Scissors • Glue stick • wire • Ribbon

📝 Steps:

1. Cut colored paper into flower petal shapes
2. Glue petals together to form flowers
3. Attach wire as stems
4. Bundle together and tie with ribbon
5. Your beautiful paper bouquet is ready!

Jewellery Making Projects

✨ Bracelet

Easy

📋 Materials Needed:

• Colorful beads • Elastic string • Scissors • Small charm (optional)

📝 Steps:

1. Cut elastic string to fit your wrist plus 2 inches
2. Thread colorful beads in any pattern you like
3. Add a charm in the middle if you want
4. Tie the ends together with a secure knot
5. Trim extra string and enjoy your bracelet!

Time: 15 minutes | Perfect for: Gifts for friends or yourself

Home Decoration Projects

🕯️Lantern

Medium

📋 Materials Needed:

• Glass mason jar • Battery LED lights • Decorative paper • Glue • Ribbon

📝 Steps:

1. Clean the mason jar thoroughly
2. Cut decorative paper to fit around the jar
3. Glue paper around the outside of jar
4. Put LED lights inside the jar
5. Tie ribbon around the top for decoration
6. Turn on lights and enjoy your lantern!

Time: 45 minutes | Perfect for: Bedroom or study table decoration

Painting Crafts Projects

🌈 Canvas Quote Painting

Easy

📋 Materials Needed:

• Small canvas • Acrylic paints • Brushes • Pencil • Eraser • Your favorite quote

📝 Steps:

1. Choose a motivational quote you love
2. Paint a colorful background on canvas
3. Let the background dry completely
4. Lightly pencil in your quote
5. Paint over the letters with contrasting color
6. Erase pencil marks and let dry

Time: 1 hour | Perfect for: Wall decoration and motivation
